Challenge Descriptions

30-Day Cardio Blast

Challenge: Complete 30 minutes of cardio exercise daily for 30 days.

Reward: Achieve a healthier heart rate, increased stamina, and a sense of accomplishment. Reward yourself with a new workout outfit or gear upon completion.

Strength Training Marathon

Challenge: Engage in a structured strength training routine for 6 weeks, focusing on major muscle groups.

Reward: Noticeable muscle toning, improved strength, and endurance. Reward yourself with a relaxing massage or spa session after completing the challenge.

Flexibility and Balance Quest

Challenge: Dedicate 20 minutes every day to stretching and balance exercises for a month.

Reward: Enhanced flexibility, better posture, and increased stability. Celebrate by treating yourself to a wellness retreat or a yoga workshop.
