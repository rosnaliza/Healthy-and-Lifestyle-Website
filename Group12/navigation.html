Holistic Health Facts

Holistic health emphasizes the importance of treating the whole person, addressing physical, mental, and spiritual well-being.
Mindfulness practices, such as meditation and yoga, are integral to holistic health approaches.
A balanced diet, regular exercise, and sufficient sleep contribute to overall well-being.
